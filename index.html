The origin of Pendulum Tuning Group

Pendulum Tuning Group was started back in 2011 towards the end of Forza Motorsport 3 with PTG Daney setting the Garage up with VXR Stu. As the team grew PTG Coops joined the team to help out with releases , and eventually when PTG Daney left it was up to Coops to run the team.

Our sole purpose back then was to concentrate on bringing cars to the community that were fairly easy to manage but coul aslo turn heads on the Leaderboards. We aimed to release a car every 3-4 weeks to the community. As time went on the PTG name started to spread throughout the Forza Motorsport world and grew into what it is today. Now we still aim to release a car on a monthly basis, hold competitions, host leagues and even more. We have plenty of very talented people in PTG, check them out on the teams section!

Who are Pendulum Tuning Garage?

Since the birth of Pendulum Tuning Group back in 2011 we have grown in one of the best community's within the virtual race world of Forza Motorsport. After starting as a team of two commited garage members we have grown into a team of approximately 20 team members. We don't limit ourselves to only playing games from the Forza Motorsport and Forza Horizon series, we are also active on other racing games; Project Cars, iRacing, as well as branching out to other game such as Battlefield and Destiny.

At Pendulum Tuning Group we pride ourselves in bringing great cars, competitions and social community events to the virtual racing community. The most important thing to us is to create a great online community where anyone is welcome to join in and have some fun with all of us. It doesn't matter is you are fast, slow, young or old, we will welcome you into our community.

Within our site you will be able to find plenty of information regarding current games we are playing, as well as upcoming release. Not to mention rexlusive releases from PTG within the Forza Motorsport would that you will only be able to find on here.

The site is constantly updated with tuning, painting, racing and drifting tips by PTG members. We host many race leagues and competitions on our site; Hot Lap Challenge, Photo Competitions and more. Winners of our forum hosted competitions can grab themselves an exclusive prize car within Forza and sometimes actual prizes from oursleves and our sponsors are up for grabs.

Be sure to drop in and say hello to us either on our discord or on xbox in party chat. We hope you enjoy your stay with us and we will see you on the racetrack.
